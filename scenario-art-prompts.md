# Scenario art prompts (Gemini / Nano Banana)

Image prompts for the 8 Tilanteet scenario characters + chat backgrounds, matching the
existing Väinö art (`frontend/public/vaino*.png`).

**Pro tip:** in Gemini, attach `vaino.png` as a reference image and start the prompt with
*"Match the art style of this reference image exactly."* — that locks the style far better
than words alone. Generate characters at 1:1, backgrounds at 16:9. For the character
stickers, ask for a plain white background and remove it afterwards (same as the Väinö set).

---

## Shared style blocks

**CHARACTER STYLE** (append to every character prompt):

> Cute flat vector cartoon character in a warm children's-book style. Soft rounded shapes
> with no outlines, shapes defined purely by color fills. Warm palette of amber, orange,
> cream and soft brown. Oversized head on a small body, round rosy blush cheeks, thick
> expressive eyebrows, simple dark glossy eyes with white highlights, friendly smile.
> A few decorative curly beige swirls floating in the air around them. Centered, full
> body, isolated on a plain white background. No text, no watermark. Square 1:1.

**BACKGROUND STYLE** (append to every background prompt):

> Flat vector cartoon scene in the same warm children's-book style: soft rounded shapes,
> no outlines, warm muted amber-brown palette, dim cozy lighting, slightly desaturated so
> chat bubbles stay readable on top. No people, no readable text or letters anywhere
> (blank signs and labels). Uncluttered composition with soft empty space in the middle.
> Landscape 16:9.

## 1. 🛒 Kauppa — Marja, the cashier

**Character:** A friendly Finnish woman in her fifties working as a grocery store cashier.
Chin-length light brown hair, cheerful warm smile, wearing an orange store apron over a
cream shirt, a small blank name badge, holding up a carton of milk in one hand and waving
with the other. + CHARACTER STYLE

**Background:** A small cozy Finnish grocery store interior: warm wooden shelves stocked
with simple blocky groceries (milk cartons, bread loaves, fruit), a checkout counter with
a small register in one corner, hanging round lamps. Blank price tags. + BACKGROUND STYLE

## 2. ☕ Kahvila — Joonas, the barista

**Character:** A relaxed young Finnish man in his twenties, a barista. Messy blond hair in
a small bun, light stubble, easy lopsided smile, wearing a brown barista apron over a
rolled-sleeve shirt, holding out a steaming coffee cup on a saucer, a cinnamon bun
(korvapuusti) balanced on his other hand. Curly steam swirls rising from the cup.
+ CHARACTER STYLE

**Background:** A cozy Helsinki café interior: a wooden counter with a pastry display case
full of round cinnamon buns, a blank chalkboard menu on the wall, an espresso machine,
warm pendant lights, a big window with soft daylight. + BACKGROUND STYLE

## 3. 🚌 Bussi — Pekka, the bus driver

**Character:** A stoic but kind Finnish bus driver in his sixties. Grey mustache, heavy
calm eyelids with a tiny hidden smile, wearing a navy-blue driver's cap and cardigan,
sitting behind a big rounded steering wheel, one hand raised in a small greeting.
+ CHARACTER STYLE

**Background:** The interior of a city bus seen from the front: rounded windshield showing
a snowy Finnish street with small colorful wooden houses, a ticket machine by the door,
empty blue seats in rows, soft evening light. Blank destination sign. + BACKGROUND STYLE

## 4. 💊 Apteekki — Liisa, the pharmacist

**Character:** A caring Finnish pharmacist in her forties. Dark hair in a neat bun, round
glasses, gentle reassuring smile, wearing a white pharmacist coat with a small green cross
on the chest pocket, holding out a small medicine box with both hands. + CHARACTER STYLE

**Background:** A tidy bright pharmacy interior: white and mint-green shelves lined with
small blocky medicine boxes and bottles, a counter in front, a round green cross symbol on
the back wall, soft clean lighting. Blank labels on everything. + BACKGROUND STYLE

## 5. 🍽️ Ravintola — Antti, the waiter

**Character:** A professional but warm Finnish waiter in his thirties. Neat dark hair,
trimmed beard, polite smile, wearing a white shirt, black waist apron and a folded cloth
over one forearm, holding a small notepad and pencil ready to take an order.
+ CHARACTER STYLE

**Background:** A casual warm restaurant interior in the evening: wooden tables with
little candles, rounded chairs, a window showing dusk-blue light outside, hanging warm
lamps, a hint of a kitchen pass in the back. Blank menu on a stand. + BACKGROUND STYLE

## 6. 🏡 Naapuri — Ritva, the neighbor

**Character:** A curious, kind retired Finnish woman in her seventies. Short curly grey
hair, big warm smile, wearing a mustard-yellow cardigan and round glasses on a cord,
holding a couple of envelopes in one hand and waving enthusiastically with the other.
+ CHARACTER STYLE

**Background:** A peaceful Finnish suburb by a row of mailboxes: two small wooden houses
(one red, one yellow) with white window frames, a low hedge, a birch tree, a gravel path,
soft summer evening light. + BACKGROUND STYLE

## 7. 🍓 Tori — Eino, the berry seller

**Character:** A cheerful old Finnish market vendor. Round nose, big grin, bushy white
eyebrows, wearing a flat cap and a red-striped market apron, proudly holding up a small
wooden basket overflowing with bright red strawberries, one strawberry held out as a free
taste. + CHARACTER STYLE

**Background:** A sunny Finnish market square (kauppatori): market stalls with orange and
cream striped awnings, crates of berries and vegetables, seagulls in the sky, a hint of
harbor and boat masts behind, bright summer morning. Blank price signs. + BACKGROUND STYLE

## 8. 🧖 Saunailta — Ossi, the friend

**Character:** An easygoing Finnish man in his early thirties relaxing in a sauna, the
learner's friend. Short sandy hair, trimmed short beard, big contented grin, bare-chested

grilling stick with a sausage (makkara) in one hand and giving a thumbs up with the other.
Curly steam swirls around him. + CHARACTER STYLE

**Background:** A warm mökki sauna interior at dusk: horizontal wooden plank walls, two
wooden bench levels, a dark stone stove (kiuas) with glowing embers, a wooden bucket and
ladle, a small window showing a lake and orange sunset, curly steam swirls near the
ceiling. + BACKGROUND STYLE
